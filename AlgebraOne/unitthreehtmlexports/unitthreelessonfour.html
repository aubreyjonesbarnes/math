Equations with Variables on Both Sides To solve an equation with a variable term on both sides, like:
Look! No more variable on this side.

To solve an equation with a variable term on both sides, like:  6x + 24 = 10x - 16 Eliminate the variable term from one side by adding its opposite to each side of the equation. 6x - 6x + 24 = 10x - 6x - 16
24 = 4x - 16
40 = 4x 
6x - 6x  + 24 = 10x - 6x - 16
6x + 24 = 10x - 16
16 + 24 = 4x - 16 + 16
10 = x
