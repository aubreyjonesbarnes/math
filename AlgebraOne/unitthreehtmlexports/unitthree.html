“Undoing” an Operation
NOTE
In science, many operations undo one another. For example, melting “undoes” freezing. Likewise, freezing “undoes” melting.
Inverse Operations
Addition and subtraction are inverse operations. This means that one operation “undoes” the other and your answer is zero.
